Common questions

Is Subnet Driller free?

Yes — completely free. No ads, no accounts, and no in-app purchases.

Does it need an internet connection?

No. Everything runs on your device. Questions are generated and graded locally — nothing is pulled from a question bank or generated by an AI model.

What does it drill?

Randomized subnetting questions using the magic-number method: network address, first and last usable host, broadcast address, and usable host counts — in both multiple-choice and written-answer formats. Every miss shows the full breakdown.

Which devices are supported?

iPhone and iPad, running a current version of iOS / iPadOS.

How do I reset my stats?

On the practice screen, tap the SESSION or TOTAL chip and confirm. That clears the matching stats; nothing leaves your device either way.

Privacy

Effective date: May 23, 2026

Subnet Driller collects nothing. The app runs entirely on your device with no network connection, no account, and no third parties.

Data collected

None. Subnet Driller does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

What stays on your device

  • Practice stats — questions answered, accuracy, and per-question-type performance. Reset anytime from the practice screen.
  • Preferences — your settings such as answer format and the optional daily reminder, stored in standard iOS/iPadOS app storage.

If you turn on the optional daily reminder, it is scheduled as a local notification on your device. No data leaves the device, and deleting the app removes everything.

What it doesn't do

  • No accounts and no login.
  • No network requests — the app is fully offline.
  • No analytics, crash reporting, or telemetry.
  • No advertising or advertising identifiers.
  • No third-party SDKs of any kind.
  • No access to location, contacts, photos, or device identifiers.

Children's privacy

Subnet Driller is rated 4+ and collects no personal information from anyone, including children.
