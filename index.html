Who made it.

I learned the magic-number method studying for a networking cert. Couldn't find a practice app that drilled it the way the real exam asks, so I built one.

The math runs live. Nothing's pulled from a question bank or generated by an LLM.

It's free.

Privacy

Effective date: May 5, 2026

Slash collects nothing. The app runs entirely on your device with no network connection, no account, and no third parties.

Data we collect

None. Slash does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

What stays on your device

  • Practice stats — total questions answered, accuracy, per-question-type performance, milestone progress, and the You're Ready badge state. Stored locally; reset anytime in Settings.
  • Calculation history — recent entries you've typed in the Calculator tab, kept locally so you can revisit them. Cleared when you tap Clear.
  • Preferences — your theme, IPv6 toggle, welcome sheet state, and other choices, stored in standard iOS/macOS app storage.

This data never leaves your device. If you delete the app, it's gone.

What it doesn't do

  • No accounts. No login.
  • No network requests. The app is fully offline.
  • No analytics, crash reporting, or telemetry.
  • No advertising or advertising identifiers.
  • No third-party SDKs of any kind.
  • No location, contacts, photos, or device identifiers.

Children's privacy

Slash does not collect personal information from anyone, including children. The app is rated 4+ and is safe for all ages.
